Dev tips:

To have the developer tools window auto open for new tabs, quite Chrome, then
re-open it by running one of the following commands in a terminal:

  On mac:
  $ open -a "Google Chrome" --args --auto-open-devtools-for-tabs

  On Windows:
  $ "C:\Program Files (x86)\Google\Chrome\Application\chrome.exe" --auto-open-devtools-for-tabs
